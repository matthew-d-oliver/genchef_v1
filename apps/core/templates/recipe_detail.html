Southwestern Chicken Kale Salad

A healthy and hearty salad featuring marinated chicken, kale, black beans, corn, and avocado, topped with a zesty lime vinaigrette.

  • 2 cups kale, chopped
  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  1. Marinate the chicken breast in olive oil, lime juice, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken on a grill or in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked, about 6-8 minutes per side. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing.In a large bowl, massage the chopped kale with a pinch of salt and olive oil until tender.
  3. Add black beans, corn, red bell pepper, and shredded cheddar cheese to the kale.
